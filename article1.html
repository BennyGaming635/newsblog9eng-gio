Is AI Here to Stay?

Is AI here to stay? Or will it just be a phase that will pop?

This is the question we ask every day regarding AI. There is a strong impact that it’s taking on our world, from governments working with companies like OpenAI to the rapid increase in workplace usage.

However, AI is also negatively impacting education. A study by HEPI showed student AI usage jumping from 66% in 2025 to 92% in 2026. This is alarming, considering these students are the future.

Students are increasingly using AI to complete work quickly, missing vital learning and bypassing the academic system.

Another major issue is the rising cost of computers. AI data centres are consuming RAM, pushing manufacturers away from consumer hardware.

RAM Price Increase

Prices for RAM have surged dramatically in recent months.

Despite negatives, AI also brings benefits. It automates repetitive and undesirable jobs.

AI allows workers to focus on meaningful tasks and spend more time with family.

AI is here to stay, and it will continue shaping the world.
